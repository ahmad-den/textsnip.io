What Data We DON'T Collect

  • Your code snippets: Never stored on our servers
  • Personal information: No names, emails, or accounts required
  • Usage analytics: We don't track how you use TextSnip
  • Browsing data: No tracking of websites you visit
  • Device information: No fingerprinting or device tracking
  • Location data: We never access your location

üéØ Bottom Line:

If you can't see it in the extension interface, we don't have access to it either.

How Your Data is Stored

TextSnip uses your browser's local storage to save your snippets. This means:

  • Local only: Data never leaves your device
  • Encrypted: Browser storage is encrypted by your operating system
  • Your control: You can export, delete, or backup anytime
  • No sync: Data isn't synchronized to any cloud service

üîê Technical Note:

We use the browser's IndexedDB API for storage, which is sandboxed and isolated from other websites and applications.

Website Analytics

Our website (textsnip.io) uses minimal, privacy-focused analytics to understand:

  • Page views and popular content (no personal identification)
  • General geographic regions (country-level only)
  • Browser types for compatibility testing

Important: This website analytics data is completely separate from the TextSnip extension and doesn't track your snippet usage.

üç™ Cookies:

We use minimal, functional cookies only. No tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

Third-Party Services

TextSnip extension operates independently and doesn't integrate with third-party services. However, our website uses:

  • Google Fonts: For typography (loaded from Google's CDN)
  • Vercel: For website hosting and performance

These services have their own privacy policies, but they don't have access to your TextSnip data.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your personal data, most traditional "data rights" don't apply. However, you have complete control:

  • Export: Download all your snippets anytime
  • Delete: Remove all data by uninstalling the extension
  • Modify: Edit or update your snippets freely
  • Transfer: Move your data to another device
